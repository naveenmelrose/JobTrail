# JobTrail

**Stop tracking job applications. Connect Gmail. Done.**

JobTrail is a free Chrome extension that automatically finds your job
applications in Gmail and organizes them on a simple dashboard — so you can
focus on getting hired instead of keeping a spreadsheet.

---

## What it does

- **Finds your applications automatically.** JobTrail reads your inbox and picks
  out job-application emails — confirmations, recruiter replies, interview
  invites — from across job boards, company sites, and recruiters.
- **Organizes them by stage.** Every application lands in a clear stage:
  Applied, In Conversation, Interview Scheduled, Awaiting Outcome, and so on.
- **Updates as new emails arrive.** Open the dashboard and JobTrail checks for
  anything new since last time.
- **Keeps everything private.** Your data stays on your own computer. JobTrail
  has no servers and never sends your information anywhere.

---

## Privacy

JobTrail is built privacy-first. It reads your Gmail **read-only** — it can
never send, delete, or change anything. There are no servers, no tracking, and
no data collection. Everything JobTrail knows about your job search lives only
on your own computer.

Read the full [Privacy Policy](privacy) for the complete details.
